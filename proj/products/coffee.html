Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our coffee products

Store coffee in an airtight container away from sunlight, heat, moisture, and strong odors. For best results, keep it in a cool, dark place (not the refrigerator or freezer, as this can introduce moisture). Ideally, buy coffee in smaller quantities and use it within 2-4 weeks of opening for maximum freshness.

Arabica beans have a sweeter, softer taste with higher acidity and more complex flavor notes (fruit, berries, nuts). They contain less caffeine and grow at higher altitudes. Robusta beans have a stronger, harsher taste with earthy notes and more caffeine (almost double). Robusta is more disease-resistant and easier to grow, often used in espresso blends for its deep flavor and crema-producing properties.

Our coffee powders are packaged in special valve-sealed bags that prevent oxygen exposure while allowing CO2 to escape. Unopened, they maintain peak freshness for up to 6 months. Once opened, we recommend consuming the coffee within 2-4 weeks for optimal flavor. The product will remain safe to consume beyond this time, but the aromatic compounds that give coffee its distinctive flavor begin to deteriorate after exposure to air.
