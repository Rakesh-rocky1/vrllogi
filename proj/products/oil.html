Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our oil products

Refined oils undergo processing to remove impurities, resulting in a neutral flavor, light color, higher smoke point, and longer shelf life. They're ideal for high-heat cooking. Unrefined oils (like extra virgin olive oil or cold-pressed coconut oil) are minimally processed, retaining more of their natural flavor, color, aroma, and nutritional components. They have lower smoke points and shorter shelf lives but offer more distinct flavors and often higher levels of beneficial compounds.

Store cooking oils in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Light, heat, and air exposure accelerate oil degradation. For delicate oils like extra virgin olive oil, consider opaque or dark glass containers that protect from light. Once opened, most oils should be used within 3-6 months for optimal quality.
